Texas Hold'em Probability Calculations

The probability of winning in Texas Hold'em depends on:

  • Your hole cards (2 cards)
  • The community cards (0-5 cards)
  • Number of opponents
  • Remaining cards in the deck

Monte Carlo Simulation

For complex scenarios, we use Monte Carlo simulation:

  1. Start with known cards (your hand + community cards)
  2. Randomly deal remaining community cards from deck
  3. Randomly deal opponents' hole cards from remaining deck
  4. Evaluate all hands and determine winner
  5. Repeat thousands of times for statistical accuracy

Win probability = (Number of wins / Total simulations) × 100%
